Policies

Simple terms.

Clear expectations keep the work clean.

Booking + cancellation

Payment is due when booking or before the session begins. 24-hour cancellation notice is required. Same-day cancellations are charged 50%, unless emergency.

Lesson slots

Weekly lesson slots are held only for package clients.

Mixing

Mixing requires a 50% deposit to start. Balance is due before final WAV delivery. Basic Song Polish includes one revision. Additional revisions are $40 each or $50/hr for live revision sessions.

Mixing files

WAV stems preferred. Vocals should be exported from the same starting point. Include the instrumental, a reference song if available, and notes on the desired emotional direction of the track.

Private groups

Private groups require a $50 deposit to reserve.

Wedding mixes

Prerecorded wedding mixes are delivered as audio files only. This is not live DJ service. Song purchases or licensing costs are billed as needed.
