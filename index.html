About my work

Music is the language of our soul.

I’m a producer, drummer, composer, recording engineer, and working musician with 20+ years in music. I studied jazz performance at Indiana University, spent years around professional studio environments including Chicago Recording Company and Classick Studios, and have over a decade of production, recording, and engineering work behind me.

I’ve played drums professionally for 15+ years across Chicago, Indiana, and the Midwest, while also working in devotional and meditative music through mrdanga, kirtan, rhythm, and voice-led group practice.

My work now moves between production, mixing, session playing, custom music, sound-based reset work, wedding mixes, and creative event architecture for people and spaces that need a clearer field.
